How It Began

We met on an ordinary afternoon that turned out to be anything but — this is where your real story goes. Lilac now shows up only as a small rule beneath the heading and a single accent block in the cornice motif above, instead of owning a whole section.

Motif is now a dentil/cornice line (echoing BPL's classical entablature) with one lilac block standing in for a single dentil — quieter and more architectural than the arch+sprig version. Story section drops the lilac wash entirely; lilac now only appears as a thin rule and that single motif accent.
